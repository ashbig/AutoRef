Analysis of the causes of false negatives in MedGene.  We compared the breast cancer-related
genes at MedGene (2359 genes) with genes listed in other databases (285 genes) and identified 26
genes that were absent from MedGene.  All of the available literature on these 26 genes was examined
manually (about 80 papers) in order to determine the reason that the gene-disease association was
missed by automated text mining.  Each source of error and the number of genes that caused that error
rate are indicated above.
